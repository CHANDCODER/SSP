Our Heritage

A legacy of purity, artistry and trust since 2009

Since 2009

Established in 2009, Sri Suvarna Silver Palace has been a trusted name in Vijayawada for exquisite silver artistry. We believe that silver is not just a metal, but a legacy passed down through generations.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide the purest 925 sterling silver with designs that balance traditional Indian heritage with modern elegance. From temple idols to antique photo frames, every piece is a masterpiece.

Located in the heart of Governerpet, Vijayawada, we welcome customers who seek authenticity, quality, and timeless beauty in every silver article we offer.
